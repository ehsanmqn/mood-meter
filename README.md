# HalNegar
The HalNegar builds emotional intelligence. Use it to:
路 Check-in regularly with your feelings
路 Expand your emotional vocabulary
路 Learn what causes you to feel the way you
路 Learn effective strategies to help you regulate your feelings
 

Authors: Ehsan Maiqani, Soroush Nasri
